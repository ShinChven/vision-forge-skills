# Environment Reference

Use these categories and keywords to inspire detailed environment translations.

## Natural Environments
- **Forest**: Primeval, temperate, tropical rainforest, boreal, redwood, deciduous, misty, sun-dappled, overgrown.
- **Desert**: Rolling sand dunes, rocky plateau, oasis, salt flats, arid, scorching, vast, sandstone pillars.
- **Mountain**: Snow-capped peaks, jagged ridges, alpine meadow, volcanic, mist-shrouded, precipice, canyon.
- **Water**: Coastal, tropical beach, stormy sea, tranquil lake, crystalline spring, frozen river, waterfall.

## Urban & Architectural
- **Cyberpunk**: Neon-lit, rain-slicked, futuristic, high-tech/low-life, holographic ads, crowded, smoggy.
- **Fantasy**: Medieval, gothic cathedral, wizard's tower, elven forest city, floating islands, steampunk.
- **Industrial**: Abandoned factory, shipyard, power plant, rust-belt, pipes and valves, brutalist.
- **Modern**: Minimalist loft, rooftop lounge, suburban street, bustling plaza, glass skyscraper.

## Sci-Fi & Otherworldly
- **Alien Planet**: Bioluminescent flora, multi-colored moons, crystalline formations, toxic atmosphere.
- **Space**: Space station interior, lunar base, cockpit view, nebula backdrop, asteroid field.
- **Post-Apocalyptic**: Overgrown ruins, scorched earth, wasteland, scavenged settlements.

## Lighting & Atmosphere
- **Lighting**: Golden hour, cinematic, moody, volumetric rays, high contrast, soft diffused, harsh neon.
- **Weather**: Foggy, thunderous, blizzard, hazy, oppressive heat, clear azure sky.
- **Composition**: Wide angle, panoramic, macro, shallow depth of field, leading lines, rule of thirds.
